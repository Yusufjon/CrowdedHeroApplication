How it works

Register and verify your account. Pass the knowledge test and submit KYC information and documents.
Learn about the risks, choose the investment project, invest, become a shareholder.
Stay connected with the lead management of your chosen company, follow their news and performance. Receive regular dividends and wait for the big Exit Day when the company is sold — then receive your profits from the sale of your shares.

Learn about the risk

Investing in startups involves high risk. So weighing such investments against the ones in mature business that are considered less risky makes just so much sense. Remember, however — all equity and debt investments are considered risky.

Majority, minority or debt?

What type of investment to choose? If, along with other investors, you have the majority in a company, your investment has more chance of being actively managed. Debt investments are offered with the accompanying minority investments — for better investment management and higher returns.

Growth markets and exit
